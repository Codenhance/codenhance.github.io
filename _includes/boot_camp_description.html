Learn by doing

Coding isn't a game, and you won't master it by typing into your browser. Learn to control and customize your development environment, manage your code, and share your projects. Write and run real code to analyze biological data.


Be social

Great software is built by teams. There's no need to hide in a corner while you learn to code -- start sharing your programs and learning about open source projects created by like-minded people.


Go at your own pace

You'll get one email per day for five days. Complete them at whatever pace works for you. Stuck? Just hit "reply" and let me help.
